- **Entry:** Individual programs at $97 (or 3x $39.99)
- **Subscription:** All Axcess at $29.99/month (40+ programs)
- **Supplements:** Own product line
- **Compelling elements:** Physical therapist credibility, injury prevention angle, lifetime access option

#### Strengths:
- Massive organic reach (14M subs, 2.4B views)

#### Weaknesses:
- **Fake weights controversy** damaged trust significantly
