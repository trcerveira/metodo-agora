## PART 1: Declaração de Missão da Escada de Valor (DMEV)

### 1. Who is your ideal client?

Men 30-50 years old. Any man who works sitting down — office, driving, reception, call center, counter, consulting. Sitting 6-12 hours/day. Most married with children. They feel their body is failing but don't know how to fix it with the life they have. They're NOT gym bros — they identify with the reality of working seated, not fitness culture.

> Full profile: `exercises/01-ideal-client-definition.md`

### 2. What result do you want to provide?

Transform desk-bound professionals from tired, stiff, overweight men who've given up on fitness into energized, pain-free, confident men who move with purpose — using just 25 minutes a day, no gym, no equipment.

### 3. DMEV (Value Ladder Mission Statement):

> **"We help desk-bound professionals get fit, pain-free, and energized through a 25-minute daily system that requires no gym and no equipment."**

**Breakdown:**
- **Who:** Men 30-50 who work sitting down
- **Result:** Fit, pain-free, and energized
- **New opportunity:** The 25-minute daily system (25 Method) — no gym, no equipment

## METHOD VALIDATION — "Segredos de um Bom Metodo" (MAV)

> Validates that the 25 Method value ladder is backed by a real method, per Icaro's "every great business has a method" framework.

### The 25 Method — Structural Checklist:

| Element | Status | Detail |
|---------|--------|--------|
| **Method has a NAME** | Yes | "25 Method" / "The 25-Minute Protocol" |
| **Method has a RECIPE** | Yes | 5 min mobility + 15 min strength + 5 min finisher |
| **Method has DIFFERENTIATION** | Yes | No gym, no equipment, 25 min, desk-worker specific |
| **Method JUSTIFIES VALUE** | Yes | Each ladder level delivers progressively more of the method |
| **Method has STORY** | Yes | Coach Teo's fall + rebuild + Richard's transformation |
| **Method has POLARITY** | Yes | "Gyms are designed for you to fail" / "You're not getting old. You're getting still." |

### 3 Sustaining Pillars — Mapped to Launch Phases:

| Pilar | Launch Phase | How It's Built |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| **Authority** | Phase 1 (Content) | Free content establishing Coach Teo as the desk-worker fitness expert |
| **Engagement** | Phase 1-3 (Content + Community) | Weekly content cadence + The 25 Club community + "25 DONE" daily check-in |
| **Social Proof** | Phase 2 (Free Trial) | Free trial completions -> testimonials -> proof for all future marketing |

### Funnel Hacking Applied to Value Ladder:

| What We Modeled | Source | Our Adaptation |
|----------------|--------|---------------|
| 7-day free trial with card required as entry | SaaS/subscription best practices | Desk-worker specific onboarding (The Ignition), auto-converts to $67/mo |
| Skool community with levels | Hormozi / community-based businesses | Military naming (Recruit -> Soldier -> Commander), commitment-based not fitness-based |
| Annual discount during trial only | SaaS urgency tactics | $497/year (38% off) only during 7-day trial — creates urgency to commit |
| 50% affiliate commission | Skool/community growth playbook | Members earn while they transform — dual hook for acquisition and retention |
| Lifetime deal for early adopters | SaaS launch strategy | $997 for first 50 members — scarcity + rewards early believers |

### Concorrencia Analisada:

| Type | Who | What We Learn |
|------|-----|--------------|
| **Direct** | Home workout programs (Athlean-X, Darebee, Fitness Blender) | Long sessions, not desk-worker specific, no community |
| **Direct** | Desk-worker fitness apps (Wakeout, Desk Yoga) | Micro-sessions, no progression, no identity/tribe |
| **Indirect** | Gym memberships (competing for the same wallet) | High dropout, anonymity, time commitment = our enemy to exploit |
| **Indirect** | Wellness/productivity apps (Headspace, Calm) | Same "busy professional" demographic, different solution |

> **Key insight:** No direct competitor combines: desk-worker specific + 25-min protocol + community with identity + progressive method + faceless brand + 50% affiliate commission. This is our differentiation.
