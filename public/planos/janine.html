ExerciseSets x RepsNotes

Foot & Knee Stability — 12 min
ExerciseSets x RepsNotes
Short foot (arch activation) Video 2x10 each foot Barefoot. "Shorten" the foot without curling toes
Ankle mobility wall drill Video 3x8 each foot Heel on ground, knee pushes toward wall
Single leg balance Video 3x20s each foot Barefoot. Eyes fixed on a point
Wall sit Video 3x20s Back flat against wall, knees at 90°
Full Body Strength — Tempo 2-2 — 28 min
ExerciseSets x RepsNotes
Goblet squat (5L bottle at chest) Video 3x10 Go as deep as possible without pain
Split squat (static) Video 3x8 each leg Hold wall if needed
Glute bridge Video 3x12 Squeeze glutes 2s at the top
Incline push-up (closed fists) Video 3x8 Fists to protect thumbs
Single arm row (bottle/backpack) Video 3x10 each arm Light grip, thumb relaxed
Dead bug Video 3x6 each side Lower back pressed into floor
Calf raises (bilateral) Video 3x12 Tempo 2-2. Ankle reinforcement
Cardio — 8 min
ExerciseFormatNotes
Brisk walking / light jog Video 2 min fast + 1 min normal ×3 Should be able to talk but with effort
Rehab — Fisioterapia — 5 min
ExerciseSets × RepsNotes
Thumb tendon glides Video 3 × 10 (all 5 positions) Straight → hook fist → full fist → tabletop → straight fist. Slow, pain-free
Eccentric wrist extension (tennis elbow) Video 3 × 10 Arm on table, palm down, wrist off edge. Lift up with other hand, slowly lower (3s) with affected arm. Small bottle (~330ml)
⚠️

Thumb pain 7/10 — rehab exercises, NOT strength. Zero pain tolerance. If pain goes above 4/10 during exercise — stop immediately.

Cool-Down — 5 min
Calf stretch against wall 30s each Video
Pigeon stretch 45s each side Video
Diaphragmatic breathing lying down 2 min Video
⚠️

Thumb pain: Push-ups always with closed fists. Rows with light grip, thumb relaxed. If any exercise causes thumb pain — stop and modify. Never push through pain.
